POPULAR DESTINATIONS

Palani

A hill station in Tamil Nadu, famous for the Palani Murugan Temple and scenic views.

Amaravathi Dam

A water reservoir on the Kaveri River, known for its peaceful surroundings and boating.

Thirumoorthy Hills

A serene hill station with a Shiva temple and beautiful trekking spots.

Ooty

A popular hill station in the Nilgiri Hills, known for its scenic landscapes, tea gardens, and cool climate.

Kodaikkanal

A charming hill station known for its lakes, waterfalls, and scenic views.

Madurai Meenakshi Amman Temple

A historic temple in Madurai, dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi, famous for its stunning architecture.

Rameswaram Temple

A sacred island with the famous Ramanathaswamy Temple, known for its religious significance and beaches.

Thanjai Periya Kovil

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this grand temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture.

Munnar

A scenic hill station in Kerala known for its tea gardens, rolling hills, and serene landscapes.

Kuttralam Falls

A beautiful waterfall in Tamil Nadu, famous for its medicinal properties and picturesque surroundings.

Thiruvannamalai

Home to the revered Arunachaleswarar Temple, this town is known for its spiritual significance and stunning landscapes.

Chidambaram Temple

Known for the famous Nataraja Temple, this ancient temple in Tamil Nadu is a masterpiece of classical art and architecture.

Kerala

A tropical paradise with backwaters, beaches, hill stations, and rich cultural heritage, known as "God's Own Country".

Bengaluru

The vibrant tech hub of India, known for its pleasant weather, parks, and rich history of innovation.

Thirupati

Famous for the Sri Venkateswara Temple, a major pilgrimage site, and a hub for religious and cultural experiences.

Pondicherry

A French colonial city with beautiful beaches, serene backwaters, and vibrant streets offering a fusion of cultures.
